Orange Search

A New Century of Privacy and Safety

Orange Search will not track your search history/change the search result manually. Natual search results and secure contents are only availble in Orange Search.

Major Concepts

1. Security

We deeply understand your desire for security. We use HTTPS/TLS1.3 to secure your data transmission

2. Privacy

We deeply understand the current desire for privacy in the society. We will not track your privacy(including your search history, your access history for other websites), and we will not sell any your personal data to orgnizations/companies

3. Search Result

We will not change the site rank manually. The site ranks are evaluated by the algorithms inside the crawler. Your search is now more natural and green.

Major Technologies

1. Crawler

Our crawler is an automatic craw engine and has a highly accurate algorithm for calculating the sites' ranks.

2. Search Results

Our server-side program uses DB_Partition_Reading technology that reduces server load (as you can exactly see on the search results page). We use your search keywords to give the search results a search weight each time, to make sure that you get the best experience on a search engine ever.
